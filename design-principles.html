Design Principles

Riqui Tellez


Contrast

Apple

www.apple.com

Apple, in my opinion, has great contrast. The colors along with the high quality images make it a really attractive website.

Alignment

Porcelanosa

www.porcelanosa.com

Porcelanosa uses square shapes making it easy to align but giving it a sense of perfectionism. I think it's great given the nature of the business.

Repetition

JCDecaux

www.jcdecaux.es

I like the way JCDecaux uses the same type of icons and buttons. I think it makes it easy to follow their concept and to perceive their offer equaly.
